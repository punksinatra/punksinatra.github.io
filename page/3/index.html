Doubleshot PBT in Carbon Colors

Yep you heard the title right. It seems like China is upping their keycap game with this new achievement.

For the most part of my mechanical keyboard life, many have asked and have wondered if it was possible to produce doubleshot PBT keycaps. 1 for one like the feel of PBT dyesubbed keycaps because of its thickness and also I like how it feels when I put my fingers on it.

Many (including signature plastics) have said that it was very hard to produce doubleshot PBT keycaps resulting to multiple dye subbed keycap sets. A chinese manufacturer may prove them wrong this time with these set. I believe it is the first one in cherry profile too.

Price is also much more affordable compared to GMK or SP doubleshot abs keycaps.

What happens next remains to be seen. Kprepublic the online seller from aliexpress has stated that this is not the first colorway that they intend to produce. Hopefully they produce support for other layouts like 75%, 1800 and ergodox. I still believe once they produce keycaps for the unusual layouts their customer base will grow exponentially.

TNC Drops 1437... Adds Armel

TNC has dropped 1437 recently and has decided to go All-Filipino for the rest of the Dota 2 season. May have guessed who would be the replacement for 1437. The only clue given was that the name starts with “A”. Many have guessed it could be Abed or abeng or ab1ng etc.

Many did not believe that it would be someone from Team Admiral since they were only recently created to compete for the rest of the season but as it turns out Armel was out of Admiral and now will be playing mid for TNC. With the new roster KuKu moves to 5th position support and will be the captain of the team.

I will have to be honest and say that I have doubts with regards to the roster change. I am not sure how long has kuku played support or if he has practiced with a lot of heroes for the 5th position but ever since I have heard of his name, he has either played the 1 position or the 2 position. Normally he plays mid and quite an aggressive one if I may say.

Frankly speaking, I think one of the weak points of TNC is their hero pool. Yeah they can play any hero but the question is how good can they play any hero. For this roster to succeed, they need to add more to their hero pool and not just adding they should know how to play other heroes properly and correctly.

In any case good luck to TNC hopefully this roster change will be effective for the remainder of the season.

Debian Minimal Desktop 01

Been using Linux since 1997 and I have fun using it full time for my home desktop computer. I wasn’t able to enjoy it before when I was younger because yeah computer games so I had to dual boot linux with windows.

Back in the day it video card and game support for linux was zero to none. Back in the day the only way to play computer games if you are running linux was via Wine but it was not really mature enough then that it takes a lot of people time to configure and run games. Not sure now about wine since I have not used it for a long time.

Things changed when nvidia started supporting the operating system by releasing their own drivers. Then came along Steam and linux native games (not that I still play a lot of games).

I posted a screenshot of my current desktop on Reddit and many seem to like it and was asking how I configured my desktop to look this way.

So I am also sharing here the things I used to make the desktop look how it should

  • Wallpaper was originally from wallhaven
  • Dock
  • Conky Config Top Bar
  • Conky MPD
  • Icons

I would expect you know how to configure conky so I wouldn’t be giving out instructions on how configured my. There are also a lot of tutorials online about conky. But if you really feel the need to ask me things you can do so by checking the homepage for ways to contact me.

The title of this post includes the number “01” since I change my desktop from time to time which means I might be posting my other desktop configuration in the future.
